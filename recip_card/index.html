Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 20 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 300g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted.
  5. Toss cooked pasta with the sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
